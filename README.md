# Upwork Profit Calculator

A lightweight, single-page calculator for freelancers to find out what they actually take home after Upwork's service fee and connects costs.

## Features

- Hourly and fixed price contract modes
- Upwork service fee input (0–15%, as shown on your proposal)
- Connects cost deduction ($0.15 each)
- Live breakdown: gross, fee deducted, connects cost, net pay
- Animated net pay counter
- Multi-currency display: USD, EUR, GBP, BAM, CAD, AUD, CHF, PLN, INR

## Usage

Enter your rate or project price, the fee % Upwork shows you on the proposal, and how many connects you spent. The calculator instantly shows your real take-home pay.

## Tech

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step.
